problem: Interactive experiences often rely on scripted content that breaks when users go off-script. Static branching does not scale to open-ended conversation.
exploration: An AI host layer where each host maintains persistent context, a consistent persona, and the ability to react to other hosts. The goal is interactive experiences that feel genuinely alive rather than triggered.
audience: Game designers, event producers, interactive storytellers, and experience builders who need dynamic conversation at scale.
model: Potential developer platform or licensing model if the host behaviour proves reliable and differentiable.
risks: Consistency across sessions, preventing personality drift, and managing host interactions that could produce unexpected outputs at runtime.
notes: The hard part is not making an AI that can talk. It is making an AI that can reliably be a specific character across many different conversations without collapsing into generic responses.
